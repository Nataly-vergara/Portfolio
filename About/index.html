Artist Statement

My practice is shaped by my pursuit of artistic stimuli through processed based work. I find these processes through material exploration. Through investigation, I discover methods and rules which create challenges to navigate. Within these set limitations there are infinite creative possibilities. This removes expectations of a certain end. My work comes to fruition when the process comes to an end.


Biography

Nataly Vergara is an undergraduate at Ohio State University, from Huntsville, Alabama. Nataly pulls motifs from observation combined with an imaginative hand. Primarily a painter, Nataly describes work with other mediums in term of their relationship to the practice of painting. This sense of materiality is indicative of her constructive process to manifest complete works.
